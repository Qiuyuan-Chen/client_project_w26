Career Exploration

“What if I am uncertain about the pathway(s) I want to pursue? How can I effectively explore career options/employers that might fit my values, skills, and overall goals?”

Not sure what career path suits you best? AI can suggest potential careers, provide insights on job market trends, and help you identify relevant skills for different industries. The key to using AI effectively is to explore multiple options, ask targeted questions about specific fields, and use AI-generated insights as a starting point for deeper research.

“My values are [insert values], some of my top skills are [insert skills], and I have interests in [insert interests]. What careers align with my values? What careers align with my skills? What careers align with my interests?”
